Radical Solutions About Page

The "Radical Solutions Platform" is an innovative project aimed at aligning with the United Nations' first Sustainable Development Goal: No Poverty. This ambitious initiative seeks to bridge the gap between education and employment for underserved communities, providing them with the tools necessary to improve their socioeconomic status.

The platform offers free skill development courses through a mobile application, empowering individuals with the knowledge and capabilities needed to access better job opportunities.

The range of courses provided by the Radical Solutions Platform is comprehensive, covering areas from vocational training in trades like carpentry and plumbing to digital literacy, coding, and entrepreneurship.
